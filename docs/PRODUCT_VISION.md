# Celestial Soul Connection — Product Vision

> Owner's brief. This is the artifact every design & build decision is measured against.
> Living document — revise deliberately, don't drift.

## 1. One-liner
**The anti–dating-app for people who are done playing.** Not a feed to grind — a small
number of genuinely-aligned souls, explained honestly, in a space calm and safe enough to
be real.

## 2. Who we build for (India-first)
Marriage-minded adults in India — including **divorced / second-chance** seekers — who want a
serious, intentional partnership, not casual swiping. Family context matters; astrology/kundli
resonates culturally; **trust and verification are decisive**. Architected so it can generalize
to global "intentional dating" later, but we win India first.

We are 18+ only. We market **soul alignment**, not explicitly "marriage."

## 3. The promise
- You will not be flooded. You get a few, well-chosen introductions.
- You will always know **why** someone was chosen for you.
- You will feel **safe** — verified people, no number-leaking, real consent, easy reporting.
- It will feel **calm and premium**, never cheap or addictive-by-design.

## 4. Design principles (the five tests)
1. **Scarcity is the feature.** Few matches, real attention. Never add an endless anything.
2. **Always explain the "why."** Every match shows its Soul Print + Star Sync reasoning. No black box.
3. **Trust before romance.** Verification, safety, consent are first-class UX — not buried settings.
4. **Astrology delights, psychology decides.** Stars are the skin; Soul Print is the spine.
   Astrology never overrides a dealbreaker or changes the core score.
5. **Every screen feels like a held breath.** Calm, warm, premium — the living-sky language
   (breathing nebula, gold stardust, the two-lights-merge). Motion means something and resolves.

## 5. The naming / concept layer (locked)
- **Soul Print** — the psychological compatibility (attachment, values, life-intent). The spine.
- **Star Sync** — the astrological layer (kundli/synastry). The celestial skin.
- Tagline: *"Matched by your Soul Print, aligned by your Star Sync."*
- Signature: *Two lights become one* — the merge animation, the emotional throughline.

## 6. The core loop (what a user actually does)
```
Arrive → feel it's different (Welcome: two lights, one sky)
  → Trust-earning onboarding (intent → Soul Print → Star Sync → first reading)
  → Daily ritual: ONE aligned soul, with the "why"
  → Open a connection (slot) → honest report → soul-probe chat
  → Mutual alignment moment → contact unlock (consented, paid) → off-app, intentionally
```

## 8. Roadmap (this pass)
**Phase 1 — Trust & first impression (P0)**
- Onboarding arc as a sacred intake with a **payoff**: end on the user's *own* first reading.
- Verification/trust surface: a visible **trust ladder** (18+ → phone → photo → ID), each with
  a plain-language "why it matters," progress, and a privacy reassurance. KYC stays stubbed.

**Phase 2 — Heartbeat & return (P1)**
- Daily-match **ritual**: anticipation → reveal → the "why" → decision. The app's pulse.
- **Who resonated with you** (premium gate) + tasteful, non-addictive nudges.

**Phase 3 — Depth (P2)**
- Life-intent alignment in the report; chat safety affordances; every empty state on-brand.

## 9. What we will NOT do (guardrails)
- No infinite swipe deck, no "X new people near you," no streaks/manipulative loops.
- No astrology that changes the compatibility score or hides a dealbreaker.
- No selling data; no number sharing in chat; no dark-pattern consent.
- No feature that we can't explain to a cautious parent of a marriage-minded user.

## 10. How we measure "world-class"
- A new user finishes onboarding feeling **safe and seen**, and gets a reading that feels *true*.
- A returning user feels **anticipation**, not obligation.
- Every screen passes the five tests in §4 and the celestial-design-system token contract.
